题目1答案：
cosA = (b²+c²-a²)/(2bc) = √5/2
±c² = b²-a²
c² = a²+b²-2abcosC
sinC·sinA = ±sinC
sinA·cosC + cosA·sinA·±sinC + ±sin2C·±sinA + sinC·cosA
-sinA = ±sin2C
±cosC + ½·±sin2C + ±cos·±sinC - ½·±sinC
cosC + sin2C + sin2C - 1 = sinC
cosC + 1 - 2sin²C + sin2C - 1 = sinC
cosC = 2sin²C
∴ sin²C/cos²C = ±1  ∴ tanC = ±1  ∴ tanC = ±√5
